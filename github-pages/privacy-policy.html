Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

Personal Information (chrome.storage.sync):

  • First Name and Last Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number and country code
  • City/Location
  • Years of experience
  • Expected salary
  • Blacklist keywords
  • Maximum years required for jobs
  • Resume/CV file (stored as Base64)

Application History (chrome.storage.local):

  • List of jobs you've applied to
  • Job titles, companies, and application dates
  • Applied and skipped counters
  • Running state (whether the bot is active)

Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

View Your Data:

All your data is visible in the extension's popup interface.

Modify Your Data:

Edit any information directly in the extension at any time.

Delete Your Data:

  • Clear all data by clicking "Reset Counters" in the extension
  • Uninstall the extension to completely remove all stored data
  • Use Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature to remove extension data

Export Your Data:

Export your application history as a CSV file using the "Export Applied Jobs" button.

Children's Privacy

EasyApplyMax is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our extension, please contact us.

Permissions Explanation

The extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

storage

Used to save your configuration and track applied jobs locally in your browser.

activeTab

Required to interact with the active LinkedIn tab when you click the Start button.

scripting

Needed to inject the automation script into LinkedIn job pages (only when you manually start the bot).

host_permissions (https://www.linkedin.com/*)

Required to access LinkedIn job pages to automate the Easy Apply process. The extension ONLY works on LinkedIn.com and has no access to other websites.
